About Us

The Groupchat Think Tank is a youth-driven innovation hub tackling pressing social and economic challenges across Rwanda and East Africa. By combining creativity, collaboration, and technology, we design solutions that impact lives and transform communities.

Our Problem Statement

Youth unemployment is one of the biggest challenges in our region, leaving many young people without sustainable career paths. We believe that tourism and agriculture hold untapped potential to create thousands of job opportunities for youth. Our mission is to bridge the gap by empowering young people with skills, resources, and access to opportunities in these sectors.

Our Mission

To empower youth through innovative programs, technology-driven platforms, and mentorship, enabling them to secure meaningful employment and create sustainable businesses in tourism and agriculture.
