About Us

The Groupchat Think Tank is a youth-led collective of innovators from Rwanda, South Sudan, and Sudan. We are united by a common purpose: to reduce youth unemployment in our communities by leveraging the power of technology, agriculture, and tourism. Our flagship platform, Ajira Hub, connects young people with career opportunities, mentorship, and access to investment networks. We believe in turning passion into purpose and ideas into action.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower youth by bridging the gap between potential and opportunity. Through platforms like Ajira Hub, we aim to create pathways for young people to access employment, grow professionally, and contribute meaningfully to their communities. By integrating technology, agriculture, and tourism, we are building a future where no youth is left behind.
