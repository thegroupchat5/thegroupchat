Ajira Hub Project

What is Ajira Hub?

Ajira Hub is a digital platform developed by The Groupchat Think Tank to address youth unemployment in Rwanda and East Africa. It connects unemployed youth with career opportunities in agriculture and tourism through an interactive and empowering ecosystem.

Key Features

  • Smart job-matching system for youth and hiring companies
  • Structured mentorship programs by industry professionals
  • Investor access and networking channels
  • Workshops, career guidance, and e-learning tools

Our Vision

To empower young people with the tools, resources, and support systems they need to build sustainable careers in key growth sectors like tourism and agriculture.
